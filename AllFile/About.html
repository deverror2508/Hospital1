ABOUTUNITY

Hospitals in Mangalore

Unity Hospitals in Mangalore renders 360 degree advanced tertiary healthcare services in Mangalore over 4 decades. A 250 bed multi- speciality Best Hospital in Mangalore offers comprehensive medical care in more than 25 specialties. Since inception in 1978, Unity Hospital has delivered outstanding value by adapting clinical and technological advancement in pursuance of creating an ecosystem of compassionate healthcare.

We have been consistently providing high-quality medical care enhancing the overall health of the community spread across coastal Karnataka and Northern Kerala. Our dedicated doctor's and the medical team put in enormous effort to draw-up curative patient-centric treatment plans for all the patients.

Unity Hospitals in Mangalore have more than 100+ consultants and 500+ employees to manage all the patients. The unwavering commitment, state-of-the-art technology and well-coordinated patient-centric team is aimed at ensuring excellence in patient care.

DERMATOLOGY AND COSMETOLOGY

The dermatology department of UNITY Hospital consists of a team of dermatologists and staff who are practicing in the field of dermatology and cosmetology for years and perform their best to treat the patients.
